Our Story

Revolutionizing the way you experience food delivery

Our Mission

At YumCart, we believe that great food should be accessible to everyone. Our mission is to connect people with the best local restaurants and provide a seamless, enjoyable food ordering experience.

We're passionate about food and technology, and we're using our expertise in both to transform how people discover, order, and enjoy their favorite meals.

Our Values

Quality First

We partner with restaurants that share our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Fast Delivery

We understand that time matters, which is why we optimize our delivery process for speed.

Sustainability

We're committed to reducing our environmental impact through eco-friendly packaging.

Community Support

We prioritize local businesses and actively contribute to the communities we serve.

Our Journey

2020

The Beginning

YumCart was founded with a vision to revolutionize food delivery. We started with just 5 restaurant partners in one city.

2021

Expansion

We expanded to 10 cities with over 500 restaurant partners and launched our mobile app.

2022

Growing Community

Our user base grew to over 1 million active users, and we introduced premium subscription services.

2023

Innovation

We implemented AI-powered recommendations and expanded to grocery delivery options.

2024

Today

We're now in 50 cities with 5000+ restaurant partners and continue to grow and innovate.
